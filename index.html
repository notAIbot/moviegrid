‚ùî Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No! MovieGrid works entirely in your browser without any signup or login required.

Where is my data stored?

All your data (Favorites, Watchlist, preferences) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No servers, no cloud storage
  • Private and secure
  • Data persists across browser sessions
  • Clearing browser data will delete your saved movies

Will my Favorites/Watchlist sync across devices?

No, because data is stored locally in your browser. Each device maintains its own separate list.

Can I use this offline?

Partially. Once movies are loaded and cached, you can view them offline. However, you need an internet connection to search for new movies or fetch fresh data from TMDB.

What if a movie isn't found?

MovieGrid searches The Movie Database (TMDB). If a movie isn't found:

  • Check the spelling and try again
  • Try the original title (not translated)
  • Try including the year (e.g., "Avatar 2009")
  • Very obscure or unreleased films may not be in TMDB yet

Can I create grids with more than 100 movies?

Yes! The Custom Grid, Favorites, and Watchlist tabs support unlimited movies. However, very large grids (200+ movies) may take longer to export as images.

What's the difference between Favorites and Watchlist?

It's up to you! Typically:

  • Favorites: Movies you've watched and love
  • Watchlist: Movies you want to watch but haven't yet

You can use them however you like - both have the same features.

How do I share my grid on Instagram?

  1. Click "üì∏ Download as Image" to save your grid
  2. Open Instagram and create a new post
  3. Upload the downloaded PNG image
  4. Add your caption and share!

Is MovieGrid free?

Yes, completely free! No ads, no premium features, no hidden costs.

üîß Troubleshooting

Movies not loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • The TMDB API may be temporarily down - try again in a few minutes
  • Clear your browser cache and reload the page

Image export is blank or incomplete

  • Wait for all posters to fully load before clicking "Download as Image"
  • For very large grids (150+ movies), be patient - it may take 30-60 seconds
  • Try using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

Action buttons (heart/clipboard) not visible

  • Hover your mouse over the movie poster to reveal buttons
  • On mobile, tap and hold the poster
  • If buttons are active (red/blue), they stay visible

Lost my Favorites/Watchlist

  • Check if you're using the same browser and device
  • Clearing browser data/cookies will delete saved lists
  • Try using the same browser profile (not incognito mode)
  • Consider downloading your grid as an image backup
